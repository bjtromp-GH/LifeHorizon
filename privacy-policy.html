The Life Horizon App is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers.

The app uses artificial intelligence models provided by Google (Generative AI) to function. When you use features that require AI processing, your text inputs are sent securely to these third-party APIs solely for the purpose of generating a response. We do not link these inputs to any identifiable user profile.

3. Permissions

The app may request certain permissions (such as network access) strictly to communicate with the AI services and function correctly on your device.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the app's official channels or the app store listing.
